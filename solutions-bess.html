BESS & Solar Solutions

KX Power is a leading developer and asset manager of utility-scale battery energy storage (BESS) and solar projects across Europe, the Middle East and Africa. Power networks and electricity markets are evolving rapidly: to meet net-zero-by-2050 targets in the UK, the EU, GCC, African and many other jurisdictions, intermittent wind and solar will need to account for almost 60% of generation by 2030. Utility-scale battery storage is therefore critical to enabling renewables to connect to — and stabilise — the grid.

We take a truly holistic approach to every project, from initial concept through to handover and beyond, including:

Design & engineering
Project management
Civil works & foundations
Electrical installation
SCADA integration
Commissioning & testing
Power trading and revenue optimization
Operation & maintenance

Our deep expertise and demonstrable track record in developing, constructing, operating and optimising energy storage and solar assets makes KX Power one of the most trusted partners in the energy transition. Whether it's enhancing grid flexibility, maximising revenue streams or securing grid connections for new renewable capacity, we deliver end-to-end solutions that de-risk your investment and accelerate your journey to net zero.
