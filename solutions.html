Integrated Energy Solutions

KX Power delivers fully integrated energy infrastructure solutions across multiple regions, seamlessly combining BESS, solar PV, and mission-critical systems under a comprehensive turnkey model. Our end-to-end approach — from feasibility and design to construction, integration, and optimized operations — enables clients to mitigate risks, maximize energy returns, and move confidently toward resilience and net zero goals.

Whether you're connecting large-scale renewables, enhancing grid flexibility or ensuring 24/7 uptime for hyperscale facilities, KX Power's holistic approach — backed by deep regional expertise and a proven track record — means you have one trusted partner for everything from BESS & Solar to data-center power.

Our Solutions

BESS & Solar

KX Power is a leading developer and asset manager of utility-scale battery energy storage (BESS) and solar projects across Europe, the Middle East and Africa. Our expertise spans the entire project lifecycle from initial concept through to operation and beyond.
