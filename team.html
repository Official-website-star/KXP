Zhe Zhang

Chief Executive Officer

"The best investment in the universe is the universe, as there are few greater things than sun, wind and tide that fill a man's dream."

Since May 2013, Dr. Zhang has been the Founder and CEO of SIFT Capital, an asset manager licensed by the Securities and Futures Commission of Hong Kong (HKSFC) (No. BBE413) and Asset Management Association of China (AMAC) (No. P1018039). Prior to that, from January 2010 to April 2013, he was an Executive Director at Goldman Sachs, where he was a member of the Supervisory Board of Goldman's Beijing Office and led multiple outbound and inbound asset/share acquisitions on behalf of clients.

Dr. Zhang is licensed as a Responsible Officer for Asset Management by HKSFC, and to practice as a professional financier by regulators in China, the UK, and the UAE.

Dr. Zhang earned a Ph.D in International Law from University of International Business and Economics (LL.D.), two master degrees in Law respectively from Peking University and Oxford University, and a bachelor degree from Shanghai Institute of Foreign Trade. He was both China Oxford Scholar and China Chevening Scholar.

Suzanne Buchta

Chief Strategist

"Innovation for good is perhaps the most inspiring of all human qualities."

Suzanne capped her 20 years career at Bank of America Merrill Lynch's NY and London offices as a Managing Director and Global Head of ESG Capital Markets & Advisory. Earlier in her career, Suzanne also acted as Head of Structured Notes and as an interest rate derivatives marketer.

One of two original co-authors of the Green Bond Principles, Suzanne was instrumental in the development of the Green Bond and Social Bond market. Starting in 2011, when her team led the first retail Green Bond, she helped to grow the market substantially by securing mandates from a broad range of multinational entities, leading Bank of America Merrill Lynch to their number one league table position for at least three consecutive years. Suzanne also helped to create one of the first Green Bond indices and to introduce the now globally recognised Green Bond Principles (GBP) to ICMA, where she served on the GBP Executive Committee and, later, the Board of ICMA, for several years. In 2015, she was awarded Financial News' 2015 Editor's Choice Award for her efforts and, in 2014, was chosen as a Bloomberg New Energy Finance "Finance Resilience" Finalist for her ambitions to "mainstream Green Bonds." Beyond Green Bonds, Suzanne regularly advised CFO's and treasury teams of multinational entities on broader ESG topics, including the impact of these risks on an entity's equity valuation and credit ratings. She also lent her expertise to European Parliament and Council Members developing financial legislation related to ESG topics.

Suzanne holds a Masters degree in Mathematics from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and was awarded a Fulbright Fellowship to study Mathematics at the University of Melbourne, Australia.

Olivia He

Chief Investment Officer

"Power drives human progress, like still waters impelling a river in a silent but impactful manner."

Olivia has over 15 years of experience in the investment banking industry. She served as Managing Director and was Global Head of Commodity Exchange Traded Products at Bank of America Merrill Lynch in London, one of the top revenue drivers in Global Commodities.

Olivia originated and spearheaded new and incremental businesses into high profile flagship multi-billion dollar global platforms with exponential revenue growth. She was responsible for initiating and executing strategic solutions and issuance, trading physical and synthetic commodity products, and managing portfolio assets in energy, metals and agriculture, with a wide variety of commodity, currency and interest-rate risk. Prior to that, she traded and structured commodity derivative products at Citigroup, and fixed income security products with a focus on structured credit and rates at UBS and RBC.

Olivia holds master degrees in both Mathematical Finance and Engineering from University of Toronto, and a bachelor degree in Engineering from Tongji University.

Irin Sun

Partner, Head of Technology

"Green energy fascinated me with an extraordinary combination of the precision of a painter and the imagination of an engineer."

Dr. Sun has over 15 years of extensive experience in mechanical engineering and the flexible optimization of power plants globally, with a proven track record in innovative technology of energy development.

From 2012 to 2020 Dr. Sun worked in GE Power AG and Alstom Power GmbH. She was responsible for the product and method development of the lifetime and condition assessment in power plant components, flexibility improvement, operation optimization and asset management of power plants in an ever evolving and growing energy market.

Prior to entering the energy sector, Dr. Sun was a scientific member for 7 years at the Technical University of Darmstadt.

Dr. Sun holds a Dr. -Ing. degree in Mechanical Engineering from Technical University of Darmstadt, a master degree in Computational Engineering from Technical University of Braunschweig, and a bachelor degree in Civil Engineering from Tongji University.

Chris Regan

Partner, Head of Power Trading

"Renewables and batteries bring you the energy you want in the power you need."

From 2017 to 2020, Chris was Head of Energy Trading Services at EDF Energy, and also served as the EDF Group Local Energy Management Director. He spearheaded EDF's Powershift business, offering flexibility trading services to batteries and gas engines, creating the UK's first battery floor price contract and growing EDF's business from first contract to market leading in under 2 years.

As an energy trader, Chris was present at two market openings before heading up EDF Energy's trading and Operations capabilities between 2009 and 2017. As Vice Chair of the FOAs Power Trading Committee and as Chair of Energy UK's Wholesale Markets subcommittee, Chris helped create the current Energy market we have today, making changes to energy trading products, negotiating Mandatory Market Making rules with OFGEM, as well as making changes to allow a customer's assets to be re-optimised closer to real time.

Prior to that, Chris was the Head of Commercial Optimisation at British Energy.

Chris holds a Master degree in Business Administration from INSEAD with a distinction, and Bachelor degree from University of Southampton.
