Who We Are

KX Power is a well-established, asset management firm specialising in the origination, development, investment and management of real, renewable energy and power generation assets. With deep knowledge of the complete value chain and entire lifecycle of renewable energy projects, KXP's local teams have successfully developed, financed, built and operated energy storage assets.

With this strong foundation and an ambition for growth, KXP have coalesced a strong pipeline for the development of future energy assets.

Our Mission

KXP is dedicated to the transition to a low-carbon energy ecosystem. With its expertise and resources in green technology, digitalisation, power trading/optimisation and project finance, KXP's vision is to become the preeminent renewable energy sponsor.

By leveraging our deep industry expertise and innovative approaches, we're committed to developing sustainable energy solutions that benefit both our clients and the planet.
